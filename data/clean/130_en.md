# New Establishment Registration

This service allows new establishments to register their commercial registrations or units in LMRA to enable them to apply work permits for expatriates, to renew and cancel their work permits.

## Service Considerations

### eService ([Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/)) Considerations

1. If the person registering the establishment is a power of attorney holder kindly attach a clear copy of the power of attorney. The type of the power of attorney should either be: The power of attorney should clearly mention the Labour Market Regulatory Authority, and the level of authorization (to submit, renew and cancel work permits).
  - General Power of Attorney for Acts and Management.
  - Power of Attorney to Manage Commercial Registrations.

2. If the employer wants to add an authorized person who has a power of attorney, kindly attach a clear copy of the power of attorney. The type of the power of attorney should either be: The power of attorney should clearly mention the Labour Market Regulatory Authority, and the level of authorization (to submit, renew and cancel work permits).
  - General Power of Attorney for Acts and Management.
  - Power of Attorney to Manage Commercial Registrations.

### Physical Location Considerations

In case of registering government and commercial entities

- Personal presence of the owner of the commercial registration or establishment is required for the completion of the registration process, or the presence of an authorized person with a power of attorney.
- If the person registering the establishment is a power of attorney holder kindly attach a clear copy of the power of attorney. The type of the power of attorney should either be: The power of attorney should clearly mention the Labour Market Regulatory Authority, and the level of authorization (to submit, renew and cancel work permits).
  - General Power of Attorney for Acts and Management.
  - Power of Attorney to Manage Commercial Registrations.

## Required Documents

**New Establishment Registration**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | ID cards of the responsible person and the authorized persons of the establishment | Copies If there is more than one authorized signatory, an appointment letter must be attached that includes the name of the responsible person and the signatures of all authorized signatories according to the commercial registry information, in addition to attaching proof of identity. |
| 2 | A printed certificate mentioning the unit number issued by the Information and e-Government Authority (IGA) (in case of unit registration) | Copy |

## Process Description

### eService ([Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/))

1. Visit the following link [Register Your Establishment](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/ToSponsorRegistration.action).
2. Fill out the required fields.
3. Upload the required documents.
4. Submit the registration.
5. A notification will appear (Your establishment registration application has been successfully submitted to LMRA, please pay the admin fees to complete the registration process) and then you will be automatically directed to the payment page.
6. After completing the admin fees payment, your registration application will be verified within 2 working days.
7. Once your establishment registration application is approved by verification, you will be able to login into the [Expat Management System (EMS](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/)) using your Advanced eKey.

### Physical Location

1. Book an appointment via [Appointment Management System (AMS).](https://ams.lmra.gov.bh/)
2. Visit Client Services Front Desk Counters at LMRA by booking an appointment for commercial services via LMRA’s [Appointment Management System](https://ams.lmra.bh/).
3. Fill out the [Establishment Registration Form PDF (1.86 MB)](https://lmra.gov.bh/files/cms/shared/establishment-registration-form-2025.pdf) and submit the required documents.
4. Once the registration application is submitted, LMRA employee will collect the admin fees from the commercial registration owner or his representative using the Point of Sale (POS) machine.
5. Once the admin fee payment is completed, you will be able to login into the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) using your Advanced eKey.

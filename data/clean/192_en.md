# Administrative Notices Removal Mechanism

The Labour Inspection Directorate inserts administrative notices on the commercial registration or on the personal number of the employer, authorized person or worker, based on organizational procedures or results of inspection visits. This service allows the employer, authorized person or worker to raise the listed administrative notice after submitting documents and completing the requirements.

## Service Conditions

1. The employer or his representative with an official power of attorney registered in LMRA system must be physically present upon submitting the service request.
2. The applicant must be the employer or the authorized person registered in the LMRA system in case of applying electronically.

## Required Documents

**Administrative Notices Removal Mechanism Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Latest electricity bill (the bill does not contain disconnection notification) | Copy |
| 2 | Photos of the establishment from inside and outside along with the commercial signboard | Copy |
| 3 | Any evidential document that proves the work volume in the establishment | Copy |
| 4 | Court ruling and Appeal certificate (in case of criminal violations) | Original |

## Process Description

### Physical Location

1. Personal attendance at the front desk of inspection offices at the Labour Market Regulatory Authority.
2. The submission of all required data and documents.

### eService (eSupport function) via the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/)

1. The employer must enter the eSupport function on the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) using his/her password.
2. Attaching all the required documents.
3. The application is processed by removing the Administrative Notices or by performing the [Remote Virtual Inspection Service (RVI)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/511).

## Legal Regulations

- [Resolution No. (74) of 2007 With Regard to Inspection on those Subject to the Provisions of the Law of Labour Market Regulation.](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/legal/show/37)

## Processing Time

- 4 working days.

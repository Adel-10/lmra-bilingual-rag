# List of (Manpower) Employment Offices

Employment offices are the offices that search for a suitable expatriate employee for an employer and facilitate the conclusion of an employment contract between the two parties, which includes hiring domestic employees and providing services to other companies and various institutions in order to employ expatriate employees to fill certain jobs at those companies or institutions.

The following is a list of Manpower agencies compliant with all laws and regulations pertaining to this business.

Notice: The LMRA calls the public to be vigilant when dealing with manpower agencies and for their own benefit. Work only with those agencies that are compliant with the laws.

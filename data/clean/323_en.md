# Appointment Management System

Appointment Management System: This system allows employers or authorized persons to book an appointment to meet with the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA's) officials. Users are also able to book an appointment for LMRA services. Finally, this system also allows employers or expatriate workers to book an appointment for biometric data collection. Appointments are available on a daily basis.

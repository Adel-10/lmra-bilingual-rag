# List of Manpower Agencies (Supplying Domestic Employees)

Licensed Manpower Supply Agencies are agencies that are allowed to supply domestic employees to work for a third party for a specific period or to complete a certain task without supervision from the employer (the supplier) on the work execution.

The following is a List of Manpower Agencies (Supplying Domestic Employees) compliant with all laws and regulations pertaining to this business.

Notice: The LMRA calls the public to be vigilant when dealing with Manpower Agencies (Supplying Domestic Employees) and for their own benefit. Work only with those agencies that are compliant with the laws.

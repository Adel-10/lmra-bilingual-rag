# Worker Registration Mechanism in Labour Registration Centres

## Service Description

The service of issuing a permit to practice a professional activity (Registered Worker) and obtaining a work card by submitting the application through the approved Labour Registration Centres.

## Registration Process

1. Worker applies for an application to obtain the permit at the approved Labour Registration Centres.
2. The Labour Registration Centre submits a permit application to the Labour Market Regulatory Authority.
3. Labour Market Regulatory Authority evaluates and audits the applications.
4. The application is transferred to the Information and eGovernment Authority and Nationality, Passports and Residence Affairs (NPRA) for verification.
5. Once the application is ready for payment, an SMS will be sent to wokers registered contact number to process the payment.
6. Payment may be made by the Registered Worker for the prescribed fees at: Registered Worker Payment Methods Payment Method SADAD online payment and all Kiosks in various areas, the Kiosks are also available in: [Approved Labour Registration Centres](https://goo.gl/maps/miwmgrrE1QPRBXss6) Cash payment: [Bahrain Financial Company (BFC)](https://goo.gl/maps/xzjuT66YpBiGPDMXA) [Lulu Exchange Co](https://luluexchange.com/bahrain/branch-locator/) Online payment channels: [BenefitPay (Fawateer service)](https://benefit.bh/personal/benefitpay/)

## Required Documents

- Filled [application form PDF (1.33 MB)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/files/cms/downloads/english_attachment/registered-worker-permit-service-form.pdf) by the worker.
- Copy of the worker's passport (must be valid).
- Official recent bank account certificate that includes the International Bank Account Number (IBAN) (if available).
- Certificate and professional qualifications approved by the applicant's embassy (If applicable).

## How to verify the Eligibility of Worker to Join the Labour Registration Centre

1. Through the [Verification of Eligibility to Join Labour Registration Program](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/checkEligibilityRW.action?methodName=loadPageInEnglish) page on the Labour Market Regulatory Authority's website.
2. Sending a text message containing your ID (CPR) number to [+973 33150150](tel:+97333150150)
3. Calling the Expatriate Services Call Centre [+973 17103103](tel:+97317103103)

## Registered Worker Permit Renewal

The registered worker can renew his/her application 6 months prior to the expiration date and has the option to renew for 6 months, one year or two years.

# Issuing New Work Permit for Domestic Employees

Through this service, the employer can apply for a new work permit for domestic employees either by submitting an electronic application via the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) or by attending in person or by proceeding to the recruitment offices or clearance agencies to avail this service.

> Note: This service is offered at a discounted rate for Senior Citizens (Bahraini employer’s aged 60 years and above). To be applied to one domestic work permit only.

## Service Conditions

1. Domestic employee passport must be valid for a period of no less than six months.
2. Medical examination must be issued by [Wafid](https://wafid.com/). If there are no accredited medical centres in the expatriate employee’s country, the employee can attend any medical centre provided that the centre itself fill out the [Non Wafid Medical Check Up Form PDF (472 KB)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/files/cms/downloads/english_attachment/medical_checkup_requirement_for_permission_work_visa_in_kingdom_of_bahrain.pdf) which will need to be attached to the domestic application during the submission.
3. Regarding domestic employees from the Republic of the Philippines, the original medical examination from the [Wafid](https://wafid.com/) list of medical centres must be submitted to LMRA.
4. Regarding domestic employees from the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia coming from one of the GCC countries, the original medical examination issued by the medical centres must be submitted.
5. You can only apply for domestic employees from the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia and Republic of Seychelles via employment offices.
6. First-degree relatives are accepted (In case of authorization only).

## Process Description

### Direct Recruitment - eService ([Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/))

1. Click [here](http://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) to login to the LMRA Expat Management System (EMS) and start the process of applying for New Work Permit application for the domestic employee.
2. Fill out the required details in the designated fields and upload the required documents.
3. Once you submit the application, an application ID will be issued, and it will be sent for verification.
4. You can check the application status (whether it has been approved or rejected) by:
  - Logging on to the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/).
  - Calling LMRA’s call centre on [+973 17506055](tel:+97317506055).

5. If approved, the employer may pay the fees through the approved channels.

### Direct Recruitment - Physical Location

1. Book an appointment via [Appointment Management System (AMS)](https://ams.lmra.gov.bh/).
2. The employer will visit the LMRA to apply for work permit.
3. Fill out the application form and attach the required documents.
4. LMRA and concerned entities will verify the application and upon approval the employer will be allowed to pay the fees.

### Employment Offices - Physical Location

1. The employer will visit the LMRA or any of the recruitment offices to apply for work permit.
2. Fill out the application form and attach the required documents.
3. LMRA and concerned entities will verify the application and upon approval the employer will be allowed to pay the fees.
4. In case of any discrepancy or need for additional documents, the application will be sent back to be rectified by the recruitment offices within 7 working days.
5. In case the application is applied by the recruitment agency, the employer will have to approve the application via the [Kingdom of Bahrain's National Portal](https://www.bahrain.bh/wps/portal/en/BNP/HomeNationalPortal/!ut/p/z1/hY5dC4IwGIV_ixfeunfkx-puFJYRRJJluwmNNY3pZJr-_UZFEPRx7t73POdwEEMpYnXWlyLrSlVn0twH5h9x5MGCTAHGSRKCT-ah6_okDrwA7f8BzNjwRRRMnt2RVwPeJgCbXUTjdTjD4OIn8KNjiZiQKn_MpXU-IgIxzc9cc-1ctXkXXde0ExtsGIbBEUoJyZ2Tqmz4FClU26H0nURNlcLFk_2KWtYNqigSSw!!/dz/d5/L0lDUmlTV0EhL3dPa0FKRnRnLzROV3FpQSEhL2Vu/) or by coming to the LMRA or by using the self-certification feature, which requires attaching two valid ID cards with the signature.

### Clearance Agencies - Physical Location

1. The employer will visit any of the clearance agencies to apply for work permit.
2. Fill out the application form and attach the required documents.
3. LMRA and concerned entities will verify the application and upon approval the employer will be allowed to pay the fees.
4. In case of any discrepancy or need for additional documents, the application will be sent back to be rectified by the clearance agencies within 7 working days.
5. In case the application is applied by the clearance agencies, the employer will have to approve the application via the [Kingdom of Bahrain's National Portal](https://www.bahrain.bh/wps/portal/en/BNP/HomeNationalPortal/!ut/p/z1/hY5dC4IwGIV_ixfeunfkx-puFJYRRJJluwmNNY3pZJr-_UZFEPRx7t73POdwEEMpYnXWlyLrSlVn0twH5h9x5MGCTAHGSRKCT-ah6_okDrwA7f8BzNjwRRRMnt2RVwPeJgCbXUTjdTjD4OIn8KNjiZiQKn_MpXU-IgIxzc9cc-1ctXkXXde0ExtsGIbBEUoJyZ2Tqmz4FClU26H0nURNlcLFk_2KWtYNqigSSw!!/dz/d5/L0lDUmlTV0EhL3dPa0FKRnRnLzROV3FpQSEhL2Vu/) or by coming to the LMRA or by using the self-certification feature, which requires attaching two valid ID cards with the signature.

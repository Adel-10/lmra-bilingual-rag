# Medical Examination Rescheduling Service for Domestic Employees

This service allows employers to update the health centre or reschedule the medical appointment for domestic employees as part of the work permit application process in the Kingdom of Bahrain.

Through this services employer will be able to:

1. Update the appointment's date and selected health centre via the [Kingdom of Bahrain’s National Portal - Medical Examination Appointments/Medical Result Service](https://services.bahrain.bh/wps/portal/en/!ut/p/z1/04_Sj9CPykssy0xPLMnMz0vMAfIjo8ziLSzcnQ2NLIy8DPw8DAwcPcy8g0ycTQ0MAs31w8EKTC2MnD28gQr8jU1MDRxNvAOdQv09jA38DfSjiNFvgAIcDZyCjJyMDQzc_Y3I0Y9sEnH68SiIwm98cGIR0IwosDJ8oYDFHFRvErKoIDc0NDSiwjPTUVERAPhw22w!/dz/d5/L0lHSkpZQSEhL3dMTUFKa0FFa0EhIS80TlZFL2Vu/), updates should be done at least one day prior to the appointment due date.
2. In case of any issues in updating, changing appointments and health centre details, employer must raise [Tawasul](https://services.bahrain.bh/wps/portal/tawasul/en/!ut/p/z1/hY9fT4NAEMQ_DY-y08XS07ctTbEEA_5JxHsxYPBKQrnmihK_vRdtTEyq7tvO_mYmS5oq0kP91pl67OxQ935_1PGTUmkyY8UZrnMFKUslF2USgRf08B-g_Rm_jMD79SfynVDcrwQiWcHrfM4o-AgkqVydL3KgWMUzSBozz9c3jE18BP4oOQUIlre8jIDUV2SkTW-br4dlaCJlSLv2pXWtC1-dl7fjuD9cBggwTVNorDV9Gz7bXYBTlq09jFT9JOmudrTfVeg2Z7p5j6YPxY4JWg!!/dz/d5/L0lHSkpZQSEhL3dMTUFOa0FFa0EhIS80TlZFL2Vu/) ticket to the Ministry of Health through the [Kingdom of Bahrain's National Portal](https://eur01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.bahrain.bh%2Fwps%2Fportal%2Fen%2FBNP%2FHomeNationalPortal%2F!ut%2Fp%2Fz1%2FhY5dC4IwGIV_ixfeunfkx-puFJYRRJJluwmNNY3pZJr-_UZFEPRx7t73POdwEEMpYnXWlyLrSlVn0twH5h9x5MGCTAHGSRKCT-ah6_okDrwA7f8BzNjwRRRMnt2RVwPeJgCbXUTjdTjD4OIn8KNjiZiQKn_MpXU-IgIxzc9cc-1ctXkXXde0ExtsGIbBEUoJyZ2Tqmz4FClU26H0nURNlcLFk_2KWtYNqigSSw!!%2Fdz%2Fd5%2FL0lDUmlTV0EhL3dPa0FKRnRnLzROV3FpQSEhL2Vu%2F&data=05%7C02%7CZaleekha.Makli%40lmra.gov.bh%7C6cbf919ba13848b1c1b108de0173000e%7Cd2c5ab7644784f7d8f1cd008d2071449%7C0%7C0%7C638949790902024144%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=v2RtaO2fPCJFOkcLuP%2F175S1r3SObRfFFoszZa12Btw%3D&reserved=0).
3. In case the appointment due date was missed, employer should Re-schedule the appointment via the [Kingdom of Bahrain’s National Portal - Medical Examination Appointments/Medical Result Service](https://eur01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fservices.bahrain.bh%2Fwps%2Fportal%2Fen%2F!ut%2Fp%2Fz1%2F04_Sj9CPykssy0xPLMnMz0vMAfIjo8ziLSzcnQ2NLIy8DPw8DAwcPcy8g0ycTQ0MAs31w8EKTC2MnD28gQr8jU1MDRxNvAOdQv09jA38DfSjiNFvgAIcDZyCjJyMDQzc_Y3I0Y9sEnH68SiIwm98cGIR0IwosDJ8oYDFHFRvErKoIDc0NDSiwjPTUVERAPhw22w!%2Fdz%2Fd5%2FL0lHSkpZQSEhL3dMTUFKa0FFa0EhIS80TlZFL2Vu%2F&data=05%7C02%7CZaleekha.Makli%40lmra.gov.bh%7C6cbf919ba13848b1c1b108de0173000e%7Cd2c5ab7644784f7d8f1cd008d2071449%7C0%7C0%7C638949790902038899%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=d95b6Nbrk%2BPwGPCaNXQcRhpTTgjMdTV7XsAyVz47LQw%3D&reserved=0).

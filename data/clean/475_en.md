# Domestic Employee Change of Occupation

Through this service, the domestic employer may change the occupation of the domestic employee.

## This Service Applies For the Following Categories:

**Domestic Employees Categories**

| No. | Male | Female |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Nurse (General) | Nurse (General) |
| 2 | Cook | Cook |
| 3 | Cook (Assistant) | Cook (Assistant) |
| 4 | Cook (Private Houses) | Cook (Private Houses) |
| 5 | Waiter (Private Service) | Waiter (Private Service) |
| 6 | Private Chauffeur | Private Chauffeur |
| 7 | Servant | Housemaid |
| 8 | Yachtsman | Nursemaid |
| 9 | Concierge in Private Residence | - |
| 10 | Gardener (House) | - |

## Special Conditions

- Obtaining Nurse (General) profession requires the employee to have a license to practice the profession from National Health Regulatory Authority (NHRA).
- Obtaining Yachtsman profession requires providing LMRA with ownership of the yacht.

## Process Description

### eService ([Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/))

1. Click [here](http://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) to login to the LMRA Expat Management System (EMS) and start the process of applying for change of occupation of the domestic employee.
2. Fill out the required details in the designated fields and upload the required documents.
3. Once you submit the application, an application ID will be issued, and it will be sent for verification.
4. You can check the application status (whether it has been approved or rejected) by:
  - Logging on to the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/).
  - Calling LMRA’s call centre on [+973 17506055](tel:+97317506055).

5. If approved, the domestic employee’s occupation will be changed in LMRA’s [Expat Management System (EMS).](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/)

### Physical Location

1. Book an appointment via [Appointment Management System (AMS)](https://ams.lmra.gov.bh/).
2. Personal attendance by the employer or the person who will act on his behalf at any of the LMRA branches with all the required documents.

### Clearance Agencies - Physical Location

1. The employer will visit any of the clearance agencies to apply for work permit.
2. Fill out the application form and attach the required documents.
3. LMRA and concerned entities will verify the application and upon approval the employer will be allowed to pay the fees.

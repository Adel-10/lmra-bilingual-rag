# Domestic Employee Absence from Work Notification

Through this service, employers can notify Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) of their domestic employee absence from work without any reason or justification.

## Service Conditions

- The work permit must be valid.
- In case the applicant is a person who will act on behalf of the employer (1st degree relative) only.
- The employee must have been absent from work without any justifiable reason or notification to the employer.
- The domestic employee must be in the Kingdom of Bahrain.

## Process Description

### eService ([Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/))

1. Submit an electronic ticket via the Submit Domestic Absence Request function under the Domestic folder on the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) by filling the required fields.
2. Once the application is applied, you will be informed via the e-ticket that the absence from work notification application has been submitted, and you must pay the admin fees via the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/).
3. Upon completion of the admin fees payment, the application will automatically be transferred for verification.
4. You can check the application status (whether it has been approved or rejected) by:
  - Calling LMRA’s call centre on [+973 17506055](tel:+97317506055).
  - Via View Domestic Absence Request under the Domestic folder on the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/).

5. If the application was approved, employer must visit the client services counters to receive the absence from work notification. Additionally, it will be sent by the Labour Marker Regulatory Authority (LMRA) electronically.

### Physical Location

1. Book an appointment via [Appointment Management System (AMS)](https://ams.lmra.gov.bh/).
2. Visit any of the branches of Labour Marker Regulatory Authority (LMRA) with the required documents.
3. Present the documents to the Client Services Directorate and pay the service fees.
4. The application will be processed and the employer will be notified by SMS once the application is processed.

# Domestic Employee Work Permit Cancellation

Through this service, the employer can cancel the domestic work permit.

## Service Conditions

- The domestic employee must have completed [biometric data collection](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/187) procedures.
- If the residence of the domestic employee to be cancelled has expired for more than one year, please contact the [Nationality, Passports and Residence Affairs (NPRA)](https://www.npra.gov.bh/en/).
- First-degree relatives are accepted (In case of authorization only).

## Process Description

### eService ([Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/))

1. Click [here](http://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) to apply for the service through the LMRA's [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) and click on the termination button to submit the termination application.
2. A notification will appear (Your termination application has been successfully submitted, kindly pay the admin fees to proceed with the transaction).
3. Once the payment is completed, you will be able to print the expatriate’s termination certificate.

### Physical Location

1. Book an appointment via [Appointment Management System (AMS)](https://ams.lmra.gov.bh/).
2. The employer or the authorized person is to visit one of the [Labour Market Regulatory Authority branches](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/95).
3. Fill out all the data in the application form and attach the required documents.
4. LMRA employee will cancel the domestic employee work permit and collect the cancellation admin fees.
5. Upon completion of the admin fees payment, LMRA employee will print the cancellation certificate and hand it to the client.

### Clearance Agencies - Physical Location

1. The employer will visit any of the clearance agencies to apply for work permit.
2. Fill out the application form and attach the required documents.
3. LMRA and concerned entities will verify the application.

# Grievance Against the Absence from Work During the Verification Period (7 Days)

Through this service, expatriate employee can file a grievance against absence from work notification during 7 days of the submission date of the application.

## Service Conditions

- Existence of a labour dispute or court case before the date of submitting the notification by the employer in the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/).
- There is an evidence of the expatriate employee’s termination of service by the employer (a letter or email to terminate the service) before or during submitting the notification.

## Required Documents

**Grievance Against the Absence from Work Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Employee Grievance Against Absence from Work During Verification Period Form PDF (1 MB) | Original |
| 2 | Passport or ID card (if available) | Copy |
| 1 | Any evidential document that proves the employee didn’t leave work, for example: Explanation letter. Any document proving that the worker has not absconded. Intention to transfer, resign or registered mail card (If any). Labour complaint or court case (If any). Police report (If any). | Copy |

## Process Description

1. The worker should attach all required documents and submit them through [Expatriate Services System](https://workers.lmra.gov.bh/).

## Legal Regulations

- [Law No. (19) of 2006 with Regard to the Regulation of the Labour Market - Article No. (28, 29)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/5).

## Processing Time

- 3 Working days.

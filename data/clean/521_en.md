# Executive Service

This service enables employers to obtain priority in submitting applications without the need to wait, to facilitate the completion of the transaction.

## Required Documents

**Executive Service Required Documents**

| No. | Document |
|---|---|
| 1 | Submit the documents based on the required service |

## Process Description

- Attend LMRA’s headquarters.
- Head to the Executive Service section and specify the required service.

## Legal Regulations

- [Resolution No. (1) for 2017 with Regard to Fees Charged in LMRA](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/legal/show/97).

## [Fees](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/473)

- Executive Service fee: BHD 20 (per transaction, fees collected when service is provided).
- Executive Virtual call fee: BHD 50 (per virtual call, fees included in the monthly bill).
- Admin fee: BHD 5 (will be charged separately for each transaction).

## Process Time

- 1 Working Day.

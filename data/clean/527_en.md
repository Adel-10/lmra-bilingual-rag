# Complaint Against Employment Office

The Complaints Follow-up department in the Client Services Directorate receives complaints from citizens and residents against authorized employment offices licensed by (LMRA) regarding the office's non-compliance with the level of service and the clauses of the agreement that have been agreed upon between the two parties.

## Service Conditions

- Availability of documents proving the contract with the employment office.
- That the contract is in place (in the probationary period / 90 days) in the event of a dispute regarding the domestic employee.
- It must be submitted by the employer personally, or his representative with an authorization to submit it.

## Process Description

### Email

1. Submit the application by sending the required documents to the email: [Complaints Section](mailto:complaints.section@lmra.gov.bh)(complaints.section@lmra.gov.bh).

Notes on the application process:

- The complaint is received by the Complaints and Follow-up Section of the Licensing Department of the Labour Market Regulatory Authority, it is reviewed and is communicated to the employment office to settle the submitted complaint.

### Physical Location

1. Book an appointment via [Appointment Management System](https://ams.lmra.gov.bh/).
2. Personal attendance at the front offices of the Licensing Department at the Labour Market Regulatory Authority
3. Fill out all the data in the application form and attach the required documents.

Notes on the application process:

- The complaint is received by the Complaints and Follow-up Section of the Licensing Department of the Labour Market Regulatory Authority, it is reviewed and is communicated to the employment office to settle the submitted complaint.

# Un-intend to Transfer

Through this service, expatriate employee can cancel his/her intention to transfer application applied earlier.

## Required Documents

**Un-intend to Transfer Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | There are no required documents |  |

## Process Description

### Physical Location

1. Fill in the [Expatriates Service Application Form PDF (1.14 MB)](https://lmra.gov.bh/files/cms/shared/expatriates-service-application-form.pdf) and submit all the required documents to the LMRA Expatriate Services Directorate.
2. Collecting the [biometric data](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/187) of the expatriate employee.

## Legal Regulations

- [Order No. (79) for 2009 Regarding the Procedures for Transferring a Foreign Worker to Another Employer.](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/legal/show/17)

## Processing Time

- Immediately.

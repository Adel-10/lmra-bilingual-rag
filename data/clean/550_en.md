# Renewal of the Registered Worker Permit

Through this service, the registered worker permit holder can renew his/ her permit six months prior to the expiry date.

## Service Conditions

- Obligation to pay the monthly fees.
- Valid passport for at least two months.

## Required Documents

**Renewal of the Registered Worker Permit Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Passport or ID card (Holder of the registered worker permit) | Copy |

## Process Description

1. Submit the renewal application to the labour registration centre or any of the collection companies (Bahrain Financial Company (BFC) or Lulu Exchange Co) and make sure that there is no pending monthly fees.
2. Registered worker should select renewal duration.
3. Application will be paid at any of LMRA payment channels.

## Legal Regulations

- [Act No. (19) of 2006 with Regard to the Regulation of the Labour Market and its Amendments.](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/5) (It is a permit issued by the Labour Market Regulatory Authority to the expatriate worker as an expatriate employer working in his name and for his own account in accordance with Articles (1, 30, 31 and 32)).

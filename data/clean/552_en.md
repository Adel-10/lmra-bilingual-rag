# Changing the Occupation of the Registered Worker

Through this service, the registered worker can be permitted to change his/ her occupation.

## Service Conditions

- The registered worker should have a valid registered worker permit.
- The registered worker should not have any administrative offences.

## Required Documents

**Changing the Occupation of the Registered Worker Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Registered Worker Permit Service Form PDF (1.33 MB) - (change of Occupation) | Copy |
| 2 | Skilled Certificates or Approval letter/ no-objection certificate from the relevant authority for certain occupations (specialized occupations only) | Copy |

## Process Description

Physical Location ([List of Approved Labour Registration Centres](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/423))

1. Filling in the [Registered Worker Permit Service Form PDF (1.33 MB)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/files/cms/downloads/english_attachment/registered-worker-permit-service-form.pdf) by the worker with his/ her signature and submitting it to the labour registration centre by attaching all the required documents after submitting the old card.
2. Must attach a copy of the Skilled Certificates or Approval letter/ no-objection certificate from the relevant authority for certain occupations (specialized occupations only).

## Legal Regulations

# Cancellation of an Expired Work Permit

Through this service, the expatriate employee can apply for a work permit cancellation after the expiry of the work permit date.

## Required Documents

**Cancellation of an Expired Work Permit Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Expatriates Service Application Form PDF (1.14 MB) | Copy |
| 2 | Any document that proves the expatriate's employee identity | Copy |
| 3 | Any document that proofs the reason of cancellation, for example: A job offer letter (in case the expatriate employee transfers to another company). Employer’s pledge (in case of changing the permit type to a domestic employee permit). Expatriate employee's pledge (in case the expatriate employee transfers to a government entity or any other entity that is not under LMRA umbrella. Travel ticket to leave the country. | Copy |

## Process Description

### eService ([Expatriate Services System](http://workers.lmra.gov.bh/))

1. The worker should attach the [Expatriates Service Application Form PDF (1.14 MB)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/files/cms/downloads/english_attachment/expatriates-service-application-form.pdf) and submit it through [Expatriate Services System](http://workers.lmra.gov.bh/).
2. Verifying the application by LMRA.
3. Receiving approval from LMRA.

## Processing Time

- Two working days.

# Cancellation of an In-Progress Application

Through this service, the expatriate employee can cancel an (in-progress application) whether it is a new work permit or renewal.

## Required Documents

**Cancellation of an In-Progress Application Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Expatriates Service Application Form PDF (1.14 MB) | Copy |

## Process Description

### eService ([Expatriate Services System](http://workers.lmra.gov.bh/))

1. The worker should attach the [Expatriates Service Application Form PDF (1.14 MB)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/files/cms/downloads/english_attachment/expatriates-service-application-form.pdf) and submit it through [Expatriate Services System](http://workers.lmra.gov.bh/).
2. Verifying the application by LMRA.
3. Receiving approval from LMRA.

## Processing Time

- One working day.

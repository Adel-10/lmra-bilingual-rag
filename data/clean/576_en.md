# Viewing Administrative Offences for Domestic Employers

Through this service, employers of domestic employees can view the listed administrative offences, and the action required to remove it.

## Required Documents

**Viewing Administrative Offences for Domestic Employers Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | There are no required documents |  |

## Process Description

### eService ([Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/))

1. Log into the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) to view the administrative offences in all statuses.
2. Click on the folder (Alert Management), then click on (View Watchlist) and select the required fields to view all the related administrative offences.

## Processing Time

- Immediately.

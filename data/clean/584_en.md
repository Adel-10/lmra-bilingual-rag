# Inspection E-Services

It is a package of electronic services provided by the Labour Inspection Directorate at the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) in order to facilitate the provision of services to clients, and ensure the speed of fulfilling their services provided electronically without the need of personal attendance at the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) building.

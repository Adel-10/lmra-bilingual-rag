# Expatriate Employee Deportation Deposit

This service allows employers to pay the deportation deposit for expatriate employees who have overstayed after the cancellation of their work permits.

## Service Conditions

- Expatriate employee work permit must be cancelled.
- The work permit cancellation date must be on or after 03-11-2023.

## Required Documents

**Expatriate Employee Deportation Deposit Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | There are no required documents |  |

## Process Description

1. Login to the LMRA [Expat Management System (EMS)](http://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) and pay expatriate employee deportation deposit.
2. Click on Billing folder, and then click on (View Overstay Workers and Deposit Payment).
3. Choose the required expatriate employee to pay the deportation deposit.
4. Click on (Pay Expat Overstay Deposit).

## Processing Time

- One working day.

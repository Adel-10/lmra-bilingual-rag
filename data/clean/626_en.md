# Domestic Deposit Refund

Through this service, expatriate employers can refund the BHD 500 domestic deposit.

## Service Conditions

- The domestic employee work permit must be exited or having an active work permit under another employer.

## Required Documents

**Domestic Deposit Refund Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Domestic Refund Application Form PDF (1.15 MB) | Copy |
| 2 | Employer International Bank Account Number ( IBAN) certificate | Original |
| 3 | Employer ID card | Copy |

## Process Description

1. Download the [Domestic Refund Application Form PDF (1.15 MB)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/files/cms/downloads/english_attachment/domestic-refund-application-form2025.pdf) and fill in the required information.
2. Submit the application via the eSupport function on the [Expat Management System (EMS)](http://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) by attaching the required documents.

## Processing Time

- 10 - 20 working days.

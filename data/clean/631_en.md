# Wage Protection System (WPS)

Welcome to the Wage Protection System 2.0

The platform that enables employers to transfer workers’ wage easily and securely through simple and reliable steps. The system provides a comprehensive reference outlining the key obligations and procedures for registration and compliance, while ensuring that wage payments are documented in a way that protects the rights of both employers and employees. By promoting transparency and accountability, the system contributes to building a fair and safe labour market that fosters trust and ensures the active participation of all stakeholders.

# Correcting a Domestic Worker's Permit for a Deceased Employer

This service allows correcting the work permit status of a domestic worker in the event of the employer's death, through one of the following two options:

- The domestic worker transfers to a new employer.
- The domestic worker departs the Kingdom of Bahrain.

## Required Documents

**Domestic Worker Permit: Deceased Employer Required Documents**

| No. | Document | Document Type |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Cancellation of Domestic Worker Due to the Employer’s Death Form PDF (1.43 MB) , whether the worker is transferred to an heir or a new employer, or the domestic worker leaves the Kingdom of Bahrain | Original |
| 2 | Domestic worker passport | Copy |

## Process Description

### Physical Location

1. Book an appointment via [Appointment Management System (AMS)](https://ams.lmra.gov.bh/).
2. The attendance of the new employer or the applicant at the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA), accompanied by the domestic worker, to submit the application.
3. The application will be processed and determined by LMRA.
4. Upon approval, the employer will be able to pay the prescribed fees and complete the procedures

## [Service Fees](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/471)

## Processing Time

- One working day.

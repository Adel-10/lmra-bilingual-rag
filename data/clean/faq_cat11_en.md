# General Questions Related to the Issuance of an International Bank Account Number (IBAN)

## 1- When is the International Bank Account Number (IBAN) Issued?

The International Bank Account Number (IBAN) is issued to every incoming expatriate employee arriving to Bahrain International Airport.

Last Update: Monday 12 August 2024

## 2- Who are the Beneficiaries from Issuing the International Bank Account Number (IBAN) Initiative?

All expatriate employees entering the kingdom of Bahrain through the Bahrain International Airport starting from August 2024.

Last Update: Monday 12 August 2024

## 3- What are the benefits of transferring wages using the International Bank Account Number (IBAN) for expatriate employees and employers?

- Safeguard the rights of work parties.
- Facilitate payment of wages through banking channels.
- Reducing judicial disputes related to the payment of wages.

Last Update: Monday 12 August 2024

## 4- How to activate the International Bank Account Number (IBAN)?

All expatriate employees arriving at Bahrain International Airport will be provided with a step-by-step explanation on how to activate their bank accounts linked to the issued International Bank Account Number (IBAN), as well as the service provider information.

Last Update: Monday 12 August 2024

## 5- Is the employer required to transfer the salary to the expatriate employee through the International Bank Account Number (IBAN) provided by the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA)?

The Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) urges employers to pay wages electronically through accredited bank accounts. To facilitate this process, Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) has started providing International Bank Account Numbers (IBAN) to all expatriate employees entering the kingdom of Bahrain through the Bahrain International Airport. This measure aims to facilitate transactions for employers and ensure documented payment records to safeguard the rights of both employers and expatriate employees.

Last Update: Monday 12 August 2024

# General Questions by Employers

## 1- How can I change the registered e-mail address?

If the responsible person wishes to change their registered email, this can be done via the [Expatriate Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/).

Last Update: Monday 6 November 2023

## 2- How can I obtain my password for my registered establishment?

You will need to obtain the [Advanced eKey](https://www.ekey.bh/bnaf-usermgmt/pages/?2&loc=en).

Last Update: Tuesday 31 October 2023

## 3- How can I reset the password used for the LMRA system?

If you forget your password, the responsible or authorized person can get his/her pin ([Advanced eKey](https://www.ekey.bh/bnaf-usermgmt/pages/jr7T7nyJDUluVQNgDr4naTA10_dtaFjX/jr7fe)) reset through the Reset Forgotten Password function.

Last Update: Tuesday 31 October 2023

## 4- Whom should I contact if there are data or visa discrepancies between EMS system and the expat’s passport?

For such issues, please use the eSupport function on the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/).

Last Update: Sunday 19 May 2024

## 5- How to make a payment?

1. Payment can be made electronically via the [Expat Management System (EMS)](http://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) through [LMRA Website](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/home).
  - Log into the [Expat Management System (EMS)](http://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) and select the establishment that you wish to pay for.
  - From the main menu, click on (Billing), then select the (Pay and View Invoices) menu.
  - In the (Transaction Type) field, select the type of transaction you want to pay for. For monthly invoices, select (Monthly), then click the (Get List) button.
  - A list of transactions requiring payment will appear. Select the desired transaction by checking the box (✔) and then click the (Pay Selected) button.
  - Confirm the amount by clicking (Confirm Payment) button.
  - Enter your credit or debit card details and then click the (Submit) button.
  - A confirmation of your payment should appear, you can print this page for future records.

2. By going to one of the banks branches accredited by LMRA, where payment is accepted either in cash or by cheque drawn on the same approved bank.

Last Update: Wednesday 26 November 2025

## 6- When does the Twenty-One-Day count for announcing the job applied for?

The Twenty-One-Day period begins after the payment of the administrative and advertisement fees.

Last Update: Wednesday 8 January 2025

## 8- What are the services that can be availed via the eSupport function on the Expat Management System (EMS)?

## Clients Services Directorate

The eSupport function on the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) can be used by employers to check and follow up on the following:

- General offences and the process to clear them.
- Bahranization calculator breakdown and percentage.
- Renewal/ transfer and termination applications rejected by the Nationality, Passport and Residence Affairs (NPRA).
- Submitted application, sent back and rejection reasons (all types of applications).
- Technical issues.
- Data or visa discrepancies between [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) and the expatriate employee details.
- Submit Local Employment Applications.
- Submit Work Permit for Golden Residency holders.
- Soft alert (Exempted the offence temporarily from Commercial Registration (CR) for different issues).
- Duplicate ID cards (CPRs).

## Workload

- Submit inquiries.
- Follow up requests applied to Corporate Labour.
- Submit Corporate Labour requests with the supporting documents.

## Applications Processing Department

- Following up and removing offences registered on the establishment and the expatriate employee.
- Following up and processing reapplied cases.
- Following up and processing issues related to visit visa status (in/out) the Kingdom of Bahrain (does not require waiting or announcing it for a period of 21 days for the employment in the Kingdom of Bahrain).
- Following up on delayed applications in the Nationality, Passport and Residence Affairs (NPRA) or in LMRA’s [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) .
- Following up and expediting applications in several cases (Submitted and sent back applications, issues related to ID card (CPR failed - CPR on hold).
- Cancelling applications before completing 21 days as per applicant’s request.
- Different inquiries about the Applications Processing Department procedures.
- Inquiries about rejection reasons from the Nationality, Passport and Residence Affairs (NPRA) or from the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA).

## Licensing Department

eSupport function on the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) can be used by employers (Employment Offices and Recruitment Manpower Agencies) and domestics employers for the following:

- Submission and following up on complaints against employment offices.
- Following up the issuance for employment offices and recruitment manpower agencies.
- Submission and following up renewal for employment offices and recruitment manpower agencies.
- Offences against (Employment offices and recruitment manpower agencies) and the process to clear them.
- Technical issues.

## Expatriate Services Directorate

- Registered workers inquiries and issues.
- Follow up on requests for workers who were unable to issue an appointment for a medical examination (Medical appointments failure issues).
- Resident permits failure issues.
- Registered workers offences.
- Inquiries and issues related to workers biometric data.
- Inquiries and issues related to matching fingerprints between more than one personal number, hit case (2 personal numbers share the same fingerprints in EMS).
- Submit Employee Absence from Work Notification.
- Soft alert (Exempted the offence temporarily from Commercial Registration (CR) for enrollment issues).
- Expatriates’ inquiries.

## Wages Protection System

The eSupport function on the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) is used to answer the clients inquires and issues related to the wage protection:

- Regular transfers/ salary transfer.
- Inaccurate assessment of data based on final settlement.
- Annual leaves.

## Labour Inspection Directorate

- [E-Settlement Service.](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/467)
- Receiving employers' requests related to the removal of administrative offences.

## Preventive Monitoring Directorate

- Following up and responding to all tickets by the employers interview team on daily basis.
- Receiving employers' requests related to the removal of administrative violations.
- Responding to inquiries and questions related to the Preventive Inspection Directorate.

## Financial Resources Directorate

- Collection:
  - Submit inquiries.
  - Deportation Deposit: BHD 300.
  - CR Credit Note Refund.
  - Domestic Credit Note Refund.

- Payment:
  - Domestic Refund Deposit: BHD 500.
  - Deportation Refund: BHD 300.
  - Labour Supply Agencies Insurance Amount Refund.

Last Update: Wednesday 16 July 2025

## 9- How to submit a new application through the eSupport function on the Expat Management System (EMS)?

1. Log into the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) using your advanced eKey.
2. Once you log in, click on the (eSupport Folder) under the main menu.
3. Click on the (Submit New Ticket) option under the eSupport folder.
4. Choose the concerned department from the list.
5. Click on (Next).
6. Enter the relevant information, depending on your request, and sufficient application details in the designated fields.
7. Click on the (Upload Documents) button and upload the required documents.
8. You may upload more than one document, once uploaded click on the (Save and Close) button.
9. Click on (Submit Ticket).

Last Update: Monday 20 May 2024

## 10- How can I follow up on a submitted application through the eSupport function on the Expat Management System (EMS)?

1. Log into the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) using your advanced eKey.
2. Once you log in, click on the (eSupport Folder) under the main menu.
3. Click on (View Tickets) option under the eSupport folder.
4. All the tickets you applied will appear, along with their updated status.
5. If the ticket status is (Await Client Response), click on the ticket ID and read what is required under the (Ticket Replies Section).
6. Once you have done the required, kindly post a reply and if you need to upload any further documents, click on the (Upload Documents) button and upload the required documents. You may upload more than one document, once uploaded click on the (Save and Close) button.
7. Click on (Submit Ticket).

Last Update: Wednesday 2 October 2024

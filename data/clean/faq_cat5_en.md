# General Inquiries about Parallel Bahrainization System

## 1- What is the Parallel Bahranization System?

It is a system designed to benefit companies requiring additional work permits for non-Bahrainis but have not reached the required Bahranization ratio qualifying them to obtain permits for non-Bahrainis. This can be done via paying an additional amount, over the issuance fee per permit out of the specified Bahrainization ratio.

The Parallel Bahranization System has been applied to new permits transactions starting from 02/05/2016 and have been applied to renewal transactions starting from 02/05/2017.

Last Update: Tuesday 16 January 2024

## 2- Is it necessary to use the Parallel Bahranization System as a condition for work permits intended for expatriate employees?

No. It is not necessary to use the Parallel Bahranization System, where you can hire Bahraini nationals and comply with the required Bahranization ratio.

Last Update: Thursday 16 November 2023

## 3- How can I find out my Bahranization commitment level?

Through [Bahrainization Target Rate Lookup Values](https://lmra.gov.bh/files/cms/shared/bahrainization-table-english%20(2).pdf) employers can find out if they meet the minimum requirement of Bahraini employment, based on the approved establishment activities.

## 4- Is there a specific fee for the Parallel Bahrainization System?

Yes, there are specific fees as shown below:

**Parallel Bahrainization System Fees**

| Service | Fees BHD |
|---|---|
| New, renewal or transfer of Work permit (for two years) Optional parallel fee for the permit out of the required Bahrainization ratio | BHD 200 BHD 500 |
| New, renewal or transfer of Work permit (for one year) Optional parallel fee for the permit out of the required Bahrainization ratio | BHD 100 BHD 250 |

These fees are not included with the medical fees.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 5- How can you use this system?

Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) clients can apply online through accessing LMRA's Electronic system, where you can find a complete set of explanatory materials in order to guide the user on how to use the system.

Businessowners and investors may also apply through the client service offices at the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) Headquarters. The Mina Salman branch will be serving clearance offices, as clients may also contact LMRA's Call Centre for any further inquiries.

Last Update: Sunday 6 October 2024

## 6- Are there any special requirements or additional documents for the use of the Parallel Bahrainization System?

There are no special requirements or additional documents, but the same requirements that are applied for issuance of the normal permit.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 7- Are there any additions in monthly fees for this type of work permits issued by the Parallel Bahrainization System?

There is no change in the monthly fees, monthly and normal fees shall apply without any change.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 8- Does the Parallel Bahranization system allow any unspecified number for work permits to be obtained and issued for expatriate employees?

The number of permits for each facility shall be linked to its activity and the size of its work.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 9- How can the renewal of work permits be issued by the Parallel Bahranization System?

The Council of Ministers' decision to issue the parallel system of Bahranization states that this system shall be applied to the renewals generally after a year of its application to the new issuance category.

The Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) system verifies if the establishment meets the required level of Bahranization ratio, if so it shall allow the client to renew the application with the usual fees and the additional fees will be applied at time of renewal for any establishment exceeding the Bahranization ratio.

Last Update: Sunday 6 October 2024

## 10- Are there specific occupations that are limited to the issuance of work permits through the Parallel Bahrainization System?

There are no specific occupations.

Last Update: Sunday 5 March 2023

## 11- What is the payment mechanism of work permits issued by the Parallel Bahranization System?

They are the same in terms of mechanism compared to the current setup being followed to pay for the issuance of work permits, including payments electronically through the electronic system of the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA), or direct payment in any of the branches of the Ahli United Bank, Al-Salam Bank or Al-Hilal Islamic Banking Services.

Last Update: Monday 7 October 2024

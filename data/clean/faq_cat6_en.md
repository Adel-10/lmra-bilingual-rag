# General Questions for Domestic Employees

## 1- What are the required documents that should be delivered to the employment office to issue work permits for domestic employees?

For more information, kindly visit the following link: [Issuing New Work Permit for Domestic Employees](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/435)

Last Update: Thursday 25 July 2024

## 2- Will I be notified about the arrival of the domestic employee?

Yes, a text message will be sent to the employer’s mobile number that is registered with Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA).

Last Update: Sunday 6 October 2024

## 3- Are there any additional steps to be taken after the arrival of the domestic employee?

1. Receiving the domestic employee’s ID card from the[Information and eGovernment Authority](https://www.iga.gov.bh/en/).
2. Completing [biometric data collection](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/187) procedures.
3. Completing [medical examination](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/439) procedures.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 4- Should the domestic employee work permit be issued through employment offices only?

No, you can also issue a work permit through any of Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) branches or Log in to LMRA [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) using the [Advanced eKey](https://www.ekey.bh/bnaf-usermgmt/pages/;jsessionid=lLwW0XhBWLA5FLwctnbP3_S9M5YwMmw_Y6JI4BcU.node1?0&loc=en).

Last Update: Monday 21 July 2025

## 5- How can I ensure that my name will not be exploited by the employment office to issue permits without my knowledge?

No work permit application can be processed without your consent, as this requires the following:

- Visiting the Authority in person to approve the application.
- The e-key of the applicant at the [Information and e-Government Authority](https://www.iga.gov.bh/en/).
- Self-certification by attaching two valid copies of the employer’s personal identity and to be signed by the employer himself as a proof of consent.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 6- Can I apply for a work permit electronically by myself?

Yes, you can apply via the [Expatriate Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/), using your [Advanced eKey](https://www.ekey.bh/bnaf-usermgmt/pages/;jsessionid=APRS4H_waq4h4udh5-ht6uO9ZJ5JfYUU58MzZ3nh.node3?0&loc=en).

Last Update: Thursday 29 August 2024

## 7- Can I recruit directly, or should it always be through the recruitment offices only?

You can either recruit directly or through an [approved employment office](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/318). In this regard, Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) warns against dealing with any unauthorized agencies or intermediaries to protect your rights.

Last Update: Sunday 6 October 2024

## 8- What are the fees?

For more information, kindly visit the following link: [Fees](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/471)

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 9- Are there any applicable monthly fees for domestic employees?

No, there are none.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 10- Should the tripartite employment contract be signed for domestic employees?

Yes, if the application is submitted through an employment office only.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 11- Is it possible to renew the work and residence permits through employment offices?

Yes, it is possible to renew the work and residence permits via the [Expatriate Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/), using the employer’s [Advanced eKey](https://www.ekey.bh/bnaf-usermgmt/pages/?1).

Last Update: Thursday 29 August 2024

## 12- Can the approved employment office submit an absence from work notice on behalf of the employer?

Yes, the approved employment office can submit an absence from work notification application on behalf of the employer after it has been authorized.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 13- Can the approved employment office terminate the domestic employee's permit on behalf of the employer?

Yes, the approved employment office can terminate the domestic employee's work permits on behalf of the employer after it being authorized.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 14- Can the approved employment office receive the fees of issuing a work permit for the domestic employees?

Yes, the approved employment office can receive the fees of issuing work permits for the domestic employees by the employer, which will then be paid on his behalf through the electronic system between the employment office and the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA).

Last Update: Sunday 6 October 2024

## 15- How can an employer review the number of domestic employees he/she has recruited?

The employer can access the [Expatriate Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) via the electronic key (eKey) and view the number of work permit details. Last Update: Thursday 25 July 2024

## 16- How can I know when the domestic employees are due for renewal?

You can log in to the [Expat Management System (EMS)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/EMS_Web/) using your advanced e-key to view the details of the domestic employees registered under you.

Also, text message will be sent to all employers once their domestic employees are due for renewal.

Last Update: Sunday 6 October 2024

## 17- Should I renew every two years?

Employers can choose to renew for a period of 6 months, a year or two years.

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

## 18- Where can I terminate a domestic employee's work or residence permit?

For more information, kindly visit the following link: [Domestic Employee Work Permit Cancellation](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/478).

Last Update: Sunday 18 February 2024

## 19- What is the method of reporting the absence from work cases?

For more information, kindly visit the following link: [Domestic Employees Absence from Work Notification](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/476).

Last Update: Sunday 18 February 2024

## 20- What are the steps should of the renewal process?

For more information, kindly visit the following link: [Renewing the Domestic Employee Work Permit](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/449).

Last Update: Sunday 18 February 2024

## 21- How can I request the local transfer of a domestic employee?

You must cancel the current work permit and then apply for a new work permit through the following services:

- [Issuing a Permanent Ceiling for Domestic Employees](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/484)
- [Issuing New Work Permit for Domestic Employees](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/435)

Last Update: Monday 7 April 2025

## 22- How can I identify approved employment offices?

A list of [approved employment offices](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/318) are available on the [Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) official website](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/home), bearing in mind that this list is updated bi-weekly by Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA).

Last Update: Sunday 6 October 2024

## 23- How can I reschedule the medical examination for the domestic employee?

For more information, kindly visit the following link: [Medical Examination Rescheduling Service for Domestic Employees](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/474)

Last Update: Tuesday 28 November 2023

# General Questions for the E-Settlement Service

## 1- How can I request a settlement if I do not have an electronic key (eKey)?

You can visit the front inspection offices at the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) main building or through virtual communication for [E-Settlement Service (Appointments)](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/628).

Last Update: Thursday 18 June 2026

## 2- Can I pay the settlement amount in installments?

No, the settlement amount must be paid in full.

Last Update: Monday 11 November 2024

## 3- What is the legal procedure if I want to withdraw my request for settlement after submitting it?

The criminal records will be referred to the Public Prosecution after the deadline for settlement has expired.

Last Update: Monday 11 November 2024

## 4- What is the legal procedure if I do not agree to the settlement?

The criminal records will be referred to the Public Prosecution and this will be recorded in the record of the proceedings.

Last Update: Monday 11 November 2024

## 5- Why should I request a settlement?

Because settlement gives the violating employer the opportunity to settle the violation at the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) before referring it to the Public Prosecution, by paying the settlement amount stipulated in the law, and thus the criminal case and all its effects expire once the settlement amount is paid in full.

Last Update: Monday 11 November 2024

## 6- How much is the settlement amount?

The settlement amount varies depending on the violation, and is multiplied by the number of violating workers.

For more information about the settlement amounts, kindly visit: [E-Settlement](https://www.lmra.gov.bh/en/page/show/467).

Last Update: Monday 11 November 2024

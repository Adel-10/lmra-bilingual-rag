# Decree (58) for the year 2019 Regarding the Appointment of Deputy CEOs in the Labour Market Regulatory Authority

Source: The King of the Kingdom of Bahrain

We, Hamad bin Isa Al-Khalifa King of the Kingdom of Bahrain After reviewing the constitution, And resolution (19) for the year 2006, regarding the regulation the labor market, and its amendments, And the Civil Service Law promulgated by decree law (48) for the year 2010, amended by decree law (69) for the year 2014, and its executive regulations promulgated by decree (51) for the year 2012, and its amendments, And decree (39) for the year 2019 regarding the organization of the Labor Market Regulatory Authority, And the proposal of the Minister of Labor and Social Development, And after the approval of the Council of Ministers,

We Have Decreed the Following: First Article The following shall be appointed in the Labor Market Regulatory Authority:

1. Ashraf Hafedh Emam Deputy CEO for Resources and Services
2. Ali Mohammed Al-Kooheji Deputy CEO for Operations and Information systems
3. Dr. Khalid Mohammed Abdulrahman Ahmed Deputy CEO for Legal Control

Second Article The Minister of Labor & Social Development should execute this decree, and it shall come into effect on the date of being published in the official gazette.

The King of the Kingdom of Bahrain Hamad bin Isa Al-Khalifa The Prime Minister Khalifa bin Salman Al-Khalifa

Issued in the Riffa Palace: Date: 26 Thu Al-Hijja 1440 H Corresponding to: 27 August 2019

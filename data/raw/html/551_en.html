Through this service, registered worker can terminate his/ her work permit.

Service Conditions

  • No pending monthly fees.
  • Payment of the termination fees if the work permit is still valid.

Required Documents

Termination of the Registered Worker Permit Required Documents
No. Document Document Type
1 Registered Worker Permit Service Form PDF (1.33 MB) Copy
2 A document proving the worker’s identity (Passport or ID card) Copy
3 Any document that proofs the reason of cancellation, for example:
  • Offer letter from another establishment (another employer).
  • Travel ticket to leave the country.
 Copy
4 Work Card Original

Process Description

  1. Filling in the Registered Worker Permit Service Form PDF (1.33 MB) and submit all the required documents.
  2. Submission of the application at the Labour Market Regulatory Authority (LMRA) system by the registration centre.
  3. Payment of termination fees, if the work permit is still valid.

Legal Regulations

  • Decree act No. (2) of 2014 Regarding Regulating Permits for Expatriate Employers to Practice Skilled Activities (Arabic Only).

Fees

  • Admin fee: BHD 5.

Payment Methods

Registered Worker Payment Methods

SADAD online payment and all Kiosks in various areas, the Kiosks are also available in:
  • Approved Labour Registration Centres
Cash payment:
  • Bahrain Financial Company (BFC)
  • Lulu Exchange Co
Online payment channels:
  • BenefitPay (Fawateer service)

Processing Time

  • Two working days.

Cooperative Entities

  • Information & eGovernment Authority (IGA).
  • Ministry of Interior - Nationality, Passport and Residence Affairs (NPRA).
